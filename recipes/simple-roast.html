Simple Roast

Image source: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/bf/Roasted_Vegetables_%2831367098615%29.jpg

Description

In keeping with the theme of the shared recipes. This recipe is super simple, possibly the simplest! This dish is perfect for the meal-prep junkies, or, if you just want to enjoy an easy roast.

Serves 3-4.

Ingredients

  • 1x Head Broccoli - cut into florets (~3cm)
  • 1x Red Onion - cut into 1/4s
  • 1x Sweet Potato - diced 2cm
  • 1 Punnet Small Tomatoes (cherry, etc.)
  • 6 Cloves Garlic - minced
  • 500gr Chicken Breasts (boneless and skinless) - diced 3cm
  • Paprika Spice
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200degC
  2. Prepare ingredients
  3. Toss all ingredients together, be liberal with seasonings and olive oil
  4. Place everything on lined baking tray and bake for 15-20min. Until slight charring on veggies and chicken is tender
  5. Serve hot with rice or side of choosing
