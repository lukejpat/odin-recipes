Creamy Salmon Pasta

Image source: https://live.staticflickr.com/2028/2309290127_dbe0fbb50b_b.jpg

Description

Simple simple simple, but delicious. This dish is brimming with full and creamy flavour.

Ingredients

  • 200gr Smoked Salmon
  • 250mL Cream (lactose-free cream works if you can't stomach dairy)
  • 300gr pasta of choice (I like fettucini)
  • 1x Onion - finely diced
  • 2x Cloves Garlic - roughly chopped
  • Neutral Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients
  2. Prepare pasta as package instructs, stop cooking and drain 1min before done. Save 1 cup pasta water
  3. Heat a splash of oil in large fry-pan over medium heat
  4. Add onions and cook until translucent (1-2min)
  5. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (1min)
  6. Add smoked salmon and smash into small pieces, cook for 30 seconds and then add cream. Bring to a slow simmer
  7. Add pasta and a splash of pasta water. If sauce looks to thick add more pasta water until desired consistency is achieved
  8. Cook for a further minute and then remove from heat.
  9. Serve hot
