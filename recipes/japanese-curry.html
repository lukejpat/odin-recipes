Japanese Curry

Image source: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/3b/Beef_curry_rice_003.jpg

Description

A delicious and homely meal, perfect for winter. This curry is mild, sweet, and full of depth. Experiment with preferred flavouring ingredients, sometimes I throw together this dish with whatever I have in the pantry. That being said there are some essentials which will be indicated below.

Serves 4.

Essential Ingredients

  • 4x blocks Japanese Curry Roux (I like Golden Sun Brand)
  • Water (or stock of choice)
  • 1x Onion - peeled and chopped into 1/4s
  • 4x Cloves Garlic - roughly chopped
  • 1cm Ginger - peeled and finely chopped
  • 1x Carrot - sliced thin diagonally
  • 500gr protein of choice (e.g. chicken, tofu, etc..)
  • 2x Potatos - peeled and chopped into 1/8s
  • Neutral Oil
  • Rice - to serve

Ingredients to Taste

The following ingredients are a matter of taste. I would recommend picking 2-3 to add as a start (or if you're really unsure, try the first three in the list ), trying to hit different flavour types (umami, sweet, salty, etc.). However, if you are confident then feel free to experiment with ingredients and quantities.

  • 2x tblsp Soy Sauce
  • 1x tblsp Honey
  • 1x Apple - diced 1-2cm
  • 2x tblsp Mirin
  • 1x tblsp Sriracha (or ketchup if you dislike heat)
  • 1x tblsp Miso Paste
  • etc.

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients
  2. Heat a dash of neutral oil in a large pot over medium/high heat
  3. Add onions and cook until translucent (1-2 min)
  4. If using chicken, add at this point and cook until cooked through
  5. Add ginger, cook until fragrant (1-2min)
  6. Add garlic, cook until fragrant (1-2min)
  7. Add carrots, potato, other protein if using, and your chosen 'ingredients to taste'. And then add water/stock to about level of the food in pot
  8. Bring to a boil, and then reduce to a simmer
  9. Simmer for 10-20min, skim foam as develops on surface
  10. Fill and rest a ladle in the curry, add 1 curry roux block and dissolve, then mix into pot. Repeat for each curry block
  11. Remove from heat and serve hot with rice
