Japanese Curry

Description

A delicious and homely meal, perfect for winter. This curry is mild, sweet, and full of depth. Experiment with preferred flavouring ingredients, sometimes I throw together this dish with whatever I have in the pantry. That being said there are some essentials which will be indicated below. Serves 4

Essential Ingredients

  • 4x blocks Japanese Curry Roux (I like Golden Sun Brand)
  • Water
  • 1x Onion - peeled and chopped into 1/4s
  • 4x Cloves Garlic - roughly chopped
  • 1cm Ginger - peeled and finely chopped
  • 1x Carrot
  • 500gr protein of choice (e.g. chicken, tofu, etc..)
  • 2x Potatos - peeled and chopped into 1/8s
  • Rice - to serve

Ingredients to Taste

The following ingredients are a matter of taste. I would recommend picking 2-3 to add as a start (or if you're really unsure, try the first three in the list ), trying to hit different flavour types (umami, sweet, salty, etc.). However, if you are confident then feel free to experiment with ingredients and quantities.

  • 2x tblsp Soy Sauce
  • 1x tblsp Honey
  • 1x Apple - diced 1-2cm
  • 2x tblsp Mirin
  • 1x tblsp Sriracha (or ketchup if you dislike heat)
  • 1x tblsp Miso Paste
  • etc.
